Omar Ibn Khattab

Decription

The life of Omar Ibn Al-Khattab, the second Caliph of Islamic State, before and after he embraces Islam.

Farouk Omar is a historical Arab series co-produced (2012) by MBC1 and Qatar TV and directed by Hatem Ali, which is based on

one of the best companions of Prophet Muhammad (Peace be upon him) and the 2nd Caliph of the Islamic state, Omar Ibn Al-

Khattab (May Allah be pleased with him)

Cast

  • Samer Ismail as Omar ibn Al-Khattab (physical actor)
  • Assad Khalifa as Omar ibn Al-Khattab (voice actor)
  • Ghassan Massoud as Abu Bakr
